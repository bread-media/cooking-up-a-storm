Synopsis

While caring for their dying patriarch, three siblings uncover a decades-old family secret. As bickering turns to chaos, their own clumsy negligence threatens to turn a quiet vigil into a fatal comedy of errors. A darkly comic meditation on family, guilt, and the chaos that erupts when love and dysfunction collide in the same kitchen.

Izz

Background

The problematic and rebellious middle child. Saw which way her life was heading — not in a good direction — and viewed a police career as her salvation and a way to erase Grandad's disappointment with most of her life choices. Like Larry, what Gramps thinks of her is an important component of her self esteem.

Aby
20s · Youngest sibling

Background

The youngest sibling but never got a free pass because of it. She lost her job as an estate agent because she was using the kitchens of the homes she was supposed to be selling to kick-start her home-chef influencer ambitions. She lost her rented flat soon after that. She now lives with Gramps and in between being his main carer she works from home as a customer service rep.

Larry

Background

Criminal solicitor/lawyer. When he started his law career he was newly married, idealistic and optimistic. He admired and looked up to Grandad and the way he was turning kids' lives around as a teacher, and wanted to do something equally important and transformational in the "justice" system. That was before crippling government cuts to legal aid and the court system made that goal a pipe dream.
